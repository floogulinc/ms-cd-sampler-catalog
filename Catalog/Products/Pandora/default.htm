Microsoft® Pandora's Box
A puzzle-solving game from Alexey Pajitnov, the creator of Tetris. Pandora's Box offers some of the most visually stimulating puzzles ever created.

Seven mythical Tricksters escape from Pandora’s Box to wreak havoc on the world. Thus begins a journey that includes 350 visually stunning puzzles. It’s up to you to solve them, capture the Tricksters, and save the world from chaos.

Puzzles are where the journey begins!
Microsoft Pandora's Box Web Site
  • Ten dazzling and unique puzzle types designed by famed Tetris creator Alexey Pajitnov.
  • Solve never-before-seen 3D puzzles, specially designed for the computer. More than 350 puzzles in all.
  • Difficulty levels to challenge every member of the family.
  • Helpful hints and Wild Cards guide you on your worldwide journey to capture the Tricksters.
  • Gameplay options allow you to follow the game’s natural story line or jump directly to your favorite puzzle.
  • Most puzzles may be solved in 15 minutes or less.
  • Manipulate pieces from back to front and top to bottom in unique 3D puzzle play.
