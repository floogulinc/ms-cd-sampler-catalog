MSN™ MoneyCentral™
MSN MoneyCentral is a complete personal finance resource that helps you manage your money and make smart financial decisions.

MoneyCentral features nine personal finance departments, with tools, information, and expert resources to help you reach your financial goals.

THE place to go to manage your money!
MSN MoneyCentral Web Site
  • Portfolio Manager - The award-winning portfolio manager lets you consolidate your investments in one place and perform powerful analysis of your holdings. Download transactions online from leading online brokers. Access your portfolio from any PC on the Web.
  • Real-time Quotes – Receive free real-time quotes on your favorite stocks.
  • Investment Finder - Find the perfect investment with this award-winning tool that lets you search in-depth databases of more than 17,000 stocks and mutual funds, applying over 500 investment criteria.
  • Advisor FYI Alerts - Personalized alerts monitor your portfolio and alert you of important events affecting your investments such as stock splits, earnings revisions, mutual fund ratings upgrades, and more.
  • News Desk – Get the latest news on your favorite stocks, industries, markets and more from leading news providers including the Wall Street Journal, TheStreet.com, Reuters, and MSNBC.
  • Department Experts - MoneyCentral features dedicated financial professionals who contribute articles, answer people’s personal questions via MoneyCentral newsgroups and provide tips and guidance in their areas of expertise.
  • Bill Pay – Receive and pay bills online with this new service, powered by the TransPoint™ Internet bill delivery and payment service.
