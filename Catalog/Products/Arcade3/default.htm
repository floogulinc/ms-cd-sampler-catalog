Microsoft® Revenge of Arcade™
Another blast of classic arcade action. Five quarter-crunchers are back with a vengeance!

Now you can relive the golden years of arcade gaming through the sights, sounds, and vengefully addictive gameplay of five arcade classics, featuring Ms. Pac-Man.

The sequel to the million-selling blockbuster hit, Return of Arcade.
Microsoft Revenge of Arcade Web Site
  • Ms. Pac-Man — Widely recognized as a better game than Pac-Man™, Ms. Pac-Man sports more complex mazes, randomly moving monsters, and more varieties of bonus snacks that bounce around the screen.
  • Rally-X — Get ready for a no-brakes search for flags through an unpredictable course of blockades and 90-degree turns.
  • Xevious — As a lone pilot in enemy airspace, it’s up to you to defend yourself against the nearly endless swarms of bad guys who attack from the ground and the air.
  • Mappy — In this playful, zany game of mouse cops and feline robbers, you must madly dash through house after house to recover stolen items.
  • Perfect for a quick break anywhere, anytime.
  • Each of the five games is easy to learn, but difficult to master.
  • You can play any of the games with a keyboard, joystick, or game pad.
  • Not replications, but actual original game code, utilizing an arcade emulator.
