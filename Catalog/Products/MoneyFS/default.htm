Microsoft® Money 2000 Deluxe
Microsoft Money 2000 Deluxe consolidates day-to-day money management with planning, investing, and online features.

Plan and achieve your financial goals with Microsoft® Money 2000 Deluxe. It offers a premier set of tools for investment research, long-term planning, and online financial management.

Manage today’s finances, achieve tomorrow’s goals!
