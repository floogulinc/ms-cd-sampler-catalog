Microsoft® Pinball Arcade
Real pinball tables - just like the ones in the arcade! 

An arcade full of tables. Seven classic pinball machines that look, sound, and play like the real thing.

Seven tables that made pinball history.
Microsoft Pinball Arcade Web Site
  • Spectacular replicas of seven classic pinball machines including the spooky tri-level Haunted House, the billiard-themed Cue Ball Wizard, and the commemorative Spirit of '76.
  • Ain't seen nothing like these stunning, 3-D graphic recreations of each table. See painstaking detail right down to reflections of the back glass.
  • A finely tuned physics model controls the ball's behavior so that every move happens just as it would on the real tables.
  • Includes one sensational table from every decade - from the '30s to the '90s.
  • Get a handle on things by nudging from the right, left, or bottom of the table.
  • Customizable controls for flippers and nudging.
  • Includes game winning strategies for pinball wizard training and an online history of pinball gaming.
