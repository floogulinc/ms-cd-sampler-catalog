Expedia.com™ Travel
Expedia.com Travel: The Right Trip at the Right Price

Expedia.com is your one stop shop on the Internet to arrange and book your travel. A free* service, Expedia.com lets you find low fares, book flights, make hotel reservations, and rent cars. Expedia.com also gives you access to a comprehensive source of travel information to help you decide where to go and what to do once you get there.

*Connect-time charges may apply
Expedia.com Travel Web Site
  • Book a Flight links customers to the Flight Wizard, which clearly outlines all the options for customers when they are reserving and purchasing a ticket:
    - Users can obtain special rates for senior travelers and kids 2-11; they can also choose to either reserve a seat for children or place them on an adult’s lap.
    - Expedia.com also offers information on the plane type, a flight’s on-time performance and connection details.
  • Fare Compare helps customers find low fares by compiling fares from actual results found on the Expedia.com travel service by other customers. In addition, it provides results across all available airlines and covers domestic as well as international routes.
  • Express Purchase allows customers to purchase airline tickets with as few as three clicks from the front page
  • Hotel Price Matcher introduces an additional hotel-shopping method to help customers get great deals on hotels. Customers state the price they are willing to pay for a room, the destination and neighborhood where they want to stay and the class of hotel they are willing to accept. Hotel Price Matcher then tries to match the customer’s requirements with the highest-class hotel possible. Once a hotel matching the customer’s criteria is found, Expedia.com makes a prepaid, nonrefundable reservation with the selected hotel.
  • The Hotel Directory enables customers to choose a hotel from more than 45,000 hotels worldwide. When customers choose a hotel, they are provided with a broad set of rates and booking options for their room(s) and can even find a hotel by searching near an address
  • Deals features exclusive promotions, vacation packages and headline news on industry sales. Through Special Deals and new Expedia Picks, customers will find the top deals from more than 30 vacation package, cruise, bed-and-breakfast, hotel and resort companies.
