Microsoft® SideWinder® Force Feedback Pro
Come to your senses! First there was sight, then there was sound, now Microsoft adds the sense of touch to revolutionize the gaming experience.

SideWinder Force Feedback Pro is a joystick that breaks new ground in game play by introducing a new sensory experience to gaming - the sense of touch, or force - to the existing senses of sight and sound found in games.
Microsoft SideWinder Web Site
  • Powerful 16-bit processor for lightning-fast forces!
  • 100 or more forces - Erase the line between you and your game. Feel as many as 100 or more forces-from subtle sensations to rugged power forces-in every game that supports force feedback technology.
  • Easy install - Install your stick easily with one simple game port connector (see system requirements).
  • Pinpoint accuracy - Meet the challenges of any game. The digital-optical camera built into Force Feedback Pro provides pinpoint accuracy, optimal speed, and no drift.
  • Forget having to recalibrate - Microsoft digital-optical technology has no moving parts, so there's no need to readjust.  
  • Less wear and tear - Get the advantage of optical parts, which move without friction so there's less wear and tear.  Play with confidence, knowing Microsoft stands behind your SideWinder Force Feedback Pro.
  • Play in comfort - Extensive usability testing makes Force Feedback Pro the clear joystick choice.
