Microsoft® Natural® Keyboard Elite
The comfortable way to type.

To design a more comfortable keyboard, Microsoft conducted extensive ergonomic and usability research. The result is the Microsoft Natural Keyboard Elite. Its split, gently sloped shape encourages a natural hand position—so you can type more comfortably.

Designed for your comfort!


Microsoft Hardware Web Site
  • Type in a relaxed posture—the Natural Keyboard Elite allows you to place your hands, wrists, and forearms in a naturally comfortable position.
  • Rest your hands in a natural position—the built-in palm rest provides a comfortable place to lay your hands when you’re not typing.
  • Organize your desktop efficiently—the Natural Keyboard Elite fits easily on your desktop and in keyboard trays.
  • Choose the most comfortable typing position by adjusting the keyboard legs to accommodate your body, chair, and desk size.
  • Take advantage of compatibility with the MS-DOS® and Windows® operating systems.
  • Save time with easy shortcuts to Windows 95 and Windows 98—open the Windows Start menu with a single touch.
  • Connect easily to any PC with a Universal Serial Bus (included in the box) or with a PS/2 port.
