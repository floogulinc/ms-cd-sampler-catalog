Microsoft® Encarta® Interactive World Atlas 2000
Explore the World From Your Desktop. Microsoft Encarta Interactive World Atlas 2000 allows you to access virtually anything on the map.

Spin the globe, zoom in for details or point to a place of interest. Dynamic 3-D maps and world cultures come to life on your PC, turning geography into a great adventure.

The award-winning multimedia world atlas!
Encarta Online Web Site
  • Find more than 1.7 million places on the map—eight times the detail of any printed atlas.
  • See the world in more than 20 dynamic map styles, including political, tectonic, ecoregions, time zones, and population density.
  • Experience the new Multimedia Map, which displays images, world music, flags, national anthems and more directly on the map.
  • Explore fascinating geologic and geographic phenomena, then trek to similar sites around the world for comparison on the new Map Treks.
  • Navigate the globe with pinpoint precision using new latitude and longitude lines you can view in any degree of detail.
  • Add or remove place names, borders and topographical details to create custom maps for reports and presentations.
  • Visit Country Contents pages for authoritative articles, local statistics, points of interest and cultural information.
