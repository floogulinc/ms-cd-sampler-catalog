Microsoft® IntelliMouse® Pro
Work more comfortably and efficiently with Microsoft IntelliMouse Pro.

Microsoft IntelliMouse Pro incorporates a new level of comfort and efficiency with its innovative design and advanced zooming and scrolling capabilities.

Engineered for comfort and efficiency.
Microsoft Pointing Devices Web Site
  • Relax your hand—IntelliMouse Pro’s shape supports your hand and fingers and its slope keeps your hand and wrist aligned in a natural position.
  • Work more comfortably—easy-to-grip rubber sides make IntelliMouse Pro comfortable to use.
  • Scroll easily by rolling the wheel forward or backward instead of clicking on a scroll bar.
  • Work hands-free with AutoScroll by simply pressing the wheel and watching your documents scroll down automatically.
  • Zoom efficiently using the wheel to see either close-up or big-picture views—without menus or toolbars in Microsoft Office 97-compatible applications.
  • Count on the Microsoft guarantee. If you’re not satisfied with your mouse for any reason, return it within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
  • Use the IntelliMouse Pro scrolling function with Windows® 95- and Windows NT® 4.0-based applications.
