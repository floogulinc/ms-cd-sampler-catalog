Microsoft® Natural® Keyboard Pro
Superior control over your PC in a comfortable  design.

The Microsoft Natural Keyboard Pro gives you one-touch access to the Internet, your e-mail, and your favorite multimedia controls. With its powerful IntelliType Pro software and built-in USB ports, the Natural Keyboard Pro combines comfort and control to let you work efficiently.

Exceptional comfort and control.


Microsoft Hardware Web Site
  • Use the Internet Hot keys to get online with the push of a button, then browse easily—right from your keyboard.
  • Check messages or open your e-mail program with the E-mail Hot Key—a single keystroke is all it takes to access your electronic mailbox.
  • Connect a USB device—directly into your keyboard through one of the two built-in USB ports.*
  • Control your music CDs and PC audio and video functions directly and easily with the Multimedia Hot Keys.**
  • Type in a relaxed posture with your hands, wrists, and forearms in a natural, comfortable position.
  • Use the My Computer and Calculator Hot Keys to quickly access these popular Windows® features. Or, customize these Hot Keys to launch your favorite programs or documents.
  • Use the Sleep Hot Key to suspend your PC, then resume work later—without having to restart your computer.†
  • Quickly perform common tasks such as Cut, Copy, and Paste with Control Key Shortcut Labels.††

†Note on using the Sleep Hot Key: Sleep key functionality depends on your operating system and PC. Windows 98 or later on a PC with Power Management capability: Provides Suspend and Resume functionality. Windows 98 or later on a PC without Power Management Capability, Windows 95, or Windows NT Workstation or Server 4.0 (Service Pack 3 or later) on any PC: Provides Screensaver, Windows Logoff, and Windows Shutdown. No Suspend/Resume functionality is provided.
