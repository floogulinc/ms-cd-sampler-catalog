Scholastic's The Magic School Bus™ Explores Inside the Earth
Become an earth expert - and have fun too!

Inspired by Scholastic Inc's award-winning book series and hit TV show, this CD-ROM software brings science concepts to life in a way that entertains, fascinates, and encourages exploration.

Recommended for kids ages 6-10
Microsoft Kids Web Site
  • Check out a volcano, explore inside a cavern, even delve inside a geode crystal! There are six different earth terrains to explore—along with games, reports, experiments, and activities—as you help Arnold find the lost pieces of his rock collection.
  • Tons of games, activities, experiments, videos, fun facts, and hundreds of clickable animations throughout the product.
  • Create your own cave paintings, grow your own geode crystals, search for fossils, or cook up some rocks and minerals in the earth kitchen.
