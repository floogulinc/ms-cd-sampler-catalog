Microsoft® Entertainment Pack - The Puzzle Collection
Power up your gray matter for a full spectrum of fun brainteasers and strategic sticklers! Games so engaging the challenge is to stop!

Check out the challenges in Microsoft Entertainment Pack— The Puzzle Collection. Play them for a minute or a marathon! Enjoy ten original games so challenging and addictive your brain will beg for more. Featuring games by Alexey Pajitnov, the Russian mastermind behind Tetris, you’ll find they’re easy to play yet tough to master!


Microsoft Entertainment Pack - The Puzzle Collection Web Site
  • Fringer™ - Untangle knots in a mad scramble against time. Fall behind and you’ll be at the end of your rope. Stay ahead and be tied up for hours.
  • Finty Flush™ - Fill the grids with the correct combination of marbles. The clock is your adversary but logic rules. Mess up and it’s gridlock.
  • Mixed Genetics - Cure bizarre mutant animals by mixing and matching common traits. Use your analytical skills and fast thinking to create genetic order out of chaos.
  • Charmer - Dancing snakes are too entranced to see their doom. Stay alert to save them before the last pot is sealed—and they’re hisssstory.
  • Check out The Puzzle Collection’s other spellbinding brainteasers: Rat Poker, Color Collision, Lineup, Spring Weekend, Jewel Chase, and Muddled Casino.
