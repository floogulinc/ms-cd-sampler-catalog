Microsoft® Publisher 2000
Quickly create professional-quality business publications.

Microsoft Publisher 2000 makes it easy to create, customize, and publish unique publications for print and online. Automated design guidance and coordinated Design Sets help you create a consistent look across all your materials. And with with the Deluxe verion which includes Microsoft Picture It!® 99 photo editing software, you can create the perfect photo to make your publications get noticed—in print and online.
