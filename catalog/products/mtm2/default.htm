Monster Truck Madness® 2
Add more rumble to your racing. Get into the mud-kicking world of gas-powered thunder with Monster Truck Madness 2.

Monster Truck Madness 2 straps you behind the wheel of 20 mechanized intimidators—awesome monster trucks let loose in the mud they were created to own. It’s smash-and-clash over 10 ferocious tracks against the toughest beasts in racing.

More Down & Dirty Racing Fun!

 
Monster Truck Madness 2 Web Site
  • Experience ground-pounding action in your choice of 20 monster trucks, including World Championship Wrestling–themed trucks like nWo Hollywood Hogan and Stinger—all with true-to-life physics models.
  • Traverse the spectacular new landscapes rendered in 16-bit color, with support for Advanced Graphics Ports and Direct3D® API-compatible graphics accelerator cards.
  • Tear up 10 unpredictable tracks plus several new multiplayer Summit Rumble venues—you can even take a run on the original Monster Truck Madness tracks!
  • Hear the roar of real monster truck engines through digitally sampled sounds, and listen to the play-by-play commentary of Army Armstrong—the voice of monster truck racing!
  • Up the competitive ante with a race in weather conditions like snow, rain, and fog—and the new nerve-rattling night racing mode!
  • Feel the power with force feedback joystick or steering wheel support, including Microsoft SideWinder® Force Feedback Pro (available separatly.)
  • Compete globally with other mad mud bullies over the MSN™ Gaming Zone, a LAN, or a modem—now with instant voice communication via your truck’s CB radio!
  • Smash, crunch, and tumble your monster truck—now these rulers of the road show real truck damage.
