Microsoft® Money 2000 Standard
Microsoft Money 2000 Standard is quick to set up and simple to use.

Microsoft Money 2000 Standard helps you quickly set up and easily perform your core financial tasks—everything from paying bills, to creating a budget, to reducing debt—in a smarter and faster way.

The easy way to manage your money!
Microsoft Money Web Site
  • Set up your Money 2000 accounts quickly with the Money Setup Assistant.
  • Track your accounts easily from your Financial Home Page, personalized with your preferred account information, common tasks, and financial reading.
  • Track all your account transactions, details, and history in one place with the electronic checkbook.
  • Pay bills online with just one click using electronic bill paying.*
  • Receive all your statements from online financial accounts with just one visit to the Internet.
  • Connect to more than 600 financial institutions online.*
  • Create a budget by forecasting expenses, then create an effective savings plan to meet your goals.

Contact your financial institution for program details. To receive and pay bills electronically through a third-party Internet Bill Delivery and Payment Provider, a checking account with any financial institution in the United States and Internet access are required, as well as other additional requirements as indicated by the third-party Internet Bill Delivery and Payment Provider. Online receipt and payment of bills through Internet Bill Delivery and Payment Providers is subject to availability by these third-party providers.
