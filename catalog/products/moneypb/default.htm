Microsoft® Money 2000 Business & Personal
The ideal financial management tool for sole proprietors.

Streamline your business and personal finances with Microsoft Money 2000 Business & Personal.  Designed specifically for sole proprietors, Microsoft Money 2000 Business & Personal provides easy tools for managing customer invoices, preparing for taxes/Schedule C, and organizing personal finances.

The Easy Way to Manage Your Business and Personal Finances
Microsoft Money Web Site
  • Keep tabs on cash flow by tracking your payables and receivables by vendor or customer.
  • Include time and reimbursable expenses on customer invoices.
  • Track inventory by products and services, including quantity and value.
  • Prepare and print comprehensive, customized statements for each customer.
  • Combine contact and financial data, such as outstanding invoices and balances due, in one convenient location with the Money Contact Manager.
  • Get automatic Schedule C categorization—Money maps your income and expenses to the correct lines of Schedule C.
  • Use Tax Estimator Plus to determine what you can expect to owe or be refunded on your quarterly federal taxes.
