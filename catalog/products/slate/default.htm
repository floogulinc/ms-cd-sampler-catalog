Slate®
Slate® online magazine combines top quality insight and analysis with the interactive and multimedia potential of the internet to give you an informed look at what matters in politics and culture.

Edited by Michael Kinsley, former co-host of CNN's "Crossfire" and longtime editor and columnist for the New Republic, Slate brings you America's leading journalists, policy makers, and cultural critics. Its articles, reviews, and forums take full advantage of Internet technology by using animation, audio and video clips, and interactive discussions.

While all of the Slate content is free* online, you can sign up for an annual subscription to get Slate premium services including:

  • any or all of eight e-mail delivery services including the popular "Today's Papers"
  • exclusive, twice-monthly chats with Slate writers.
  • A $10 gift certificate good for merchandise in the Slate Company Store.
