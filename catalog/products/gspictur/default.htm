Microsoft® Picture It!® 2000
Make good pictures great pictures!

Fun, easy, and powerful, Picture It! 2000 consumer photo-editing software makes you look like a pro! Picture It! 2000 makes photo editing so easy that anyone can create professional-quality cards, calendars, collages and more!

Make good pictures look great…and have more fun using them!
Microsoft Picture It! Web Site
  • Capture your photo from any source: digital camera, scanner, floppy disk, or the Internet.
  • Picture It! 2000 guides you through every step and gives you lots of graphic elements to play with: photos, frames, backgrounds, textures, and more.
  • Crop, cut, rotate, or flip photos. Alignment guides and rulers help you create cool collages with ease.
  • Easily edit out anything from red eye to telephone wires. Touch up all or part of any photo—even replace entire backgrounds.
  • Restore old photos by repairing scratches, tears, and dust. Or add stamps, paint, special effects or fancy (even 3-D) text.
  • After fixing and enhancing your photos, use backgrounds, clip art, text fonts, and templates to create cards, calendars, and much more.
  • Print your project on your color printer or add it to your Internet e-mail or Web page. Web wizards will help you create and post personal Web pages, slideshows and albums.
