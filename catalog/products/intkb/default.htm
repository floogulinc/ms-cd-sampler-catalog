Microsoft® Internet Keyboard
Easy Internet access and control from your keyboard.

10 Hot Keys let you take control of your computing. The Internet Keyboard gives you a high-quality design with the tools and easy Web access to help you work more efficiently.

One-touch access to the Internet and more!


Microsoft Hardware Web Site
  • Get online with the push of a button, then browse right from your keyboard.
  • Open your e-mail program to check messages with the E-mail Hot Key—a single keystroke is all it takes to access your electronic mailbox.
  • Quickly perform common tasks such as Cut, Copy, and Paste with the help of Control Key Shortcut Labels.*
  • Get one-touch access to popular Windows features such as My Computer or the Calculator. Or, customize these Hot Keys to launch your favorite programs, documents, or Web sites.
  • Use the Sleep Hot Key to suspend your PC, then resume work later—without having to restart your computer.**
  • Use the palm rest to rest your palms or hands while not typing.
