Microsoft® IntelliMouse® TrackBall
Precision and comfort in a very smart design. Improve the way you work with Microsoft IntelliMouse TrackBall.

Microsoft IntelliMouse TrackBall is designed for comfort and efficiency. Its unique shape fits your hand and supports it in a natural position. Simply roll the wheel to scroll instead of clicking on a scroll bar, or work hands free with the AutoScroll feature.

  • Experience comfort and accuracy with precise fingertip control.
