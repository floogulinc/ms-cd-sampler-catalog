Microsoft® Bookshelf® 2000
Write and communicate with power and precision! Microsoft Bookshelf 2000 helps you create better documents of all kinds—and it works effectively with Microsoft Office.

Microsoft Bookshelf 2000 CD-ROM reference library gives you fast, easy access to the reference resources you need to create better letters, papers, presentations, Web sites, e-mail, and more. And it works effectively with Microsoft Office to help you communicate better everywhere.

Powerful resources for persuasive communicators!
Microsoft Bookshelf Web Site
  • Communicate more effectively in your documents, presentations, Web sites, and e-mail with a wealth of powerful tools: word definitions, synonyms, quotes, encyclopedia and Web resources, writing guidelines, historical timelines, and more.
  • Increase your productivity with fast access to the newest versions of trusted reference works such as The American Heritage® Dictionary of the English Language, Third Edition; The Original Roget’s Thesaurus of English Words and Phrases; and The Encarta® Manual of Style and Usage—all integrated with extensive cross-links.
  • Store the dictionary and thesaurus on your hard disk for faster searching with the Bookshelf HD feature.
  • Get one-click access to all nine Bookshelf reference works with the QuickShelf® shortcut icon.
  • Find just the word you need among 250,000 synonyms by clicking the QuickSynonym icon.
  • Search the entire Columbia Dictionary of Quotations by clicking the QuickQuote icon.
