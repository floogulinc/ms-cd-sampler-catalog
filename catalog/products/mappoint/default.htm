Microsoft® MapPoint™ 2000

Manage your business better with comprehensive mapping and analysis tools. Identify business trends and illustrate key information with Microsoft MapPoint 2000.

Microsoft MapPoint 2000 business mapping software lets you easily integrate data with many Microsoft Office applications into detailed maps—enabling you to visually analyze the information you need to stay competitive.

The Microsoft Office mapping and analysis program.


Microsoft MapPoint Web Site
  • Create maps that combine vital business data with demographic information, a detailed legend, and drawing tools for easy map customization.
  • Easily insert customized maps into Microsoft Office applications—including Microsoft Publisher documents—or share maps across a company intranet or on the Internet.
  • Easily download the maps to handheld PCs for mobile professionals on the road.
  • Simplify the creation and presentation of shaded maps with Data Mapping Wizards to reflect relevant company data from Microsoft Excel, Outlook®, Access, and SQL Server™.
  • Keep your map overlays and analyses accurate and up-to-date using some of the best and most versatile local-area demographic data from Claritas, Inc.
  • Easy-to-find locations and addresses in the United States are based on the premier map database from Geographic Data Technologies, Inc. (GDT), the largest supplier of domestic cartographic data.
