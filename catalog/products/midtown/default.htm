Midtown Madness™
Say goodbye to gridlock.

Here’s your chance to careen your way through Chicago traffic and forget everything you learned in Driver’s Ed! No-rules racing means doing whatever it takes to win. With five unique racing modes, ten unique cars, and wide-open, unbridled, pedal-to-the-metal racing, Midtown Madness is the only antidote for rush-hour blues.

Now that’s racing Chicago style!
Midtown Madness Web Site
  • Five ways to race:
    1) Beat the clock as you blitz down boulevards and back alleys
    2) Charge your way through checkpoint races
    3) Blow lap-racing wide open via city-centered circuit races
    4) Drive without competition and learn every back-alley shortcut in Cruising Mode
    5) Race online or challenge opponents in a cops and robbers multiplayer chase
  • Realistically modeled driving performance for ten unique cars
  • A top-speed tour of Chicago — complete with landmarks and hundreds of interactive objects to plow into
  • Set the weather and time-of-day, traffic conditions, pedestrian density, and number of cops on the road
  • Realistic damage models and real-time interactive police network
  • In-game commentary by Chicago radio personality Marty Lennartz, "The Regular Guy"
  • Multiple viewpoints including in-car with dash and chase views
