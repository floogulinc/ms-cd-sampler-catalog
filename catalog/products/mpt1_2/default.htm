Microsoft® My Personal Tutor 1st & 2nd Grade
Unlock your child’s potential with all the essential skills for 1st and 2nd grade with My Personal Tutor!

Come along with Professor Presto on a fun-filled learning journey, from outer space to under the sea to a museum brimming with adventure. The fun and learning never stop with My Personal Tutor, the only comprehensive, grade-based solution with TutorAssist™ learning technology. Just like a tutor, this groundbreaking technology identifies your child’s specific learning needs and offers instruction where and when it’s needed most.

Recommended for kids ages 6 to 8
Microsoft Kids Web Site
  • Join the adventure! Sam the Monkey and his animal friends love to play hide-and-seek in a wild and wacky museum. Help Sam find his friends while mastering essential 1st and 2nd grade reading skills.
  • Blast off to the stars! Sky, the Jet-Powered Skateboarder, lives on a space station where a mischievous alien stowaway has been creating havoc with all the space station’s systems. Save the station while learning key 1st and 2nd grade math skills.
  • Set sail with Turru the Pelican on a daring mission! Turru and his friends need your help saving captured sea creatures. Develop your decision-making and thinking skills as you save the sea creatures and guide them home.
  • How TutorAssist technology encourages learning success
    Assesses    : As your child learns a new concept, such as double-digit subtraction, TutorAssist recognizes if help is needed.
    Instructs    : Whether your child needs a quick course correction or a more extended explanation, Professor Presto is standing by on every screen, ready to leap into an activity to lend a hand with just the right level of help. Your child keeps on playing and learning without a break in the action.
    Inspires Confidence: With constant help and reinforcement from TutorAssist learning technology, there’s no limit to what your child can learn and achieve.
