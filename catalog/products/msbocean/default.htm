Scholastic's The Magic School Bus™ Explores the Ocean
Join Ms. Frizzle and her class for an amazing undersea adventure!

Inspired by Scholastic Inc.'s award-winning book series and hit TV show, this CD-ROM software is designed to entertain and encourage a sense of adventure and exploration. Discover strange creatures on the ocean floor, conduct science experiments, delve into multimedia reports, play fun games and find the missing treasure.

Recommended for kids ages 6-10
Microsoft Kids Web Site
  • Science is fun and easier to understand with real videos, informative games and hundreds of clickable objects that reveal fun facts about the ocean.
  • Explore seven different ocean zones as you follow clues to find the hidden treasure! Put on your diving gear to explore the ocean on your own.
  • Conduct seven different experiments that teach scientific concepts in a fun and easy-to-understand way. Learn how to create the perfect wave for Liz to surf upon. Understand how fish must change to live in different environments.
  • Play any of the seven games or five activities for hours of fun!
