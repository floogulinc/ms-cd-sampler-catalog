Microsoft® IntelliMouse® with IntelliEye™
The Microsoft IntelliMouse with IntelliEye optical technology uses a revolutionary optical sensor—no mouse ball needed!

The improved version of the popular IntelliMouse pointing device replaces the rubber-ball mechanics of a traditional mouse with an optical sensor that scans your desktop 1,500 times per second for pinpoint accuracy.

A major advance in mouse technology.


Microsoft Pointing Devices Web Site
  • A sealed optical sensor means no moving parts to clean or wear out.
  • The precise sensor detects motion on hundreds of surfaces, including wood, plastic, or even your pant leg.
  • The sculpted shape gives you comfortable support, and the curved sides make it easy to hold with either hand.
  • The IntelliEye sensor has no moving parts to clean, so your mouse stays smooth and precise.
  • The IntelliMouse with IntelliEye comes with a five-year warranty.
