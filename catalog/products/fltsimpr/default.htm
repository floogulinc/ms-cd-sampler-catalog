Microsoft® Flight Simulator 2000: Professional Edition
The simulation of choice for pilots and and serious aviation enthusiasts!

If you take the controls of just one flight simulation this year, make it this one. For over 17 years, Microsoft Flight Simulator has defined quality and realism. Now the bar for flying simulations has been raised once again. For the first time ever, Microsoft has created a new edition of Flight Simulator designed specifically for pilots and serious aviators like you.

Microsoft Flight Simulator 2000 Professional Edition – the most technologically advanced Flight Simulator ever created.
Microsoft Flight Simulator Web Site
  • Developed with the leaders in the aviation industry, Professional Edition puts you in command of a fleet of aircraft that ranges from a single-engine Cessna to the supersonic Concorde. Fly anywhere in the entire world — to more than 20,000 airports … over scenic mountains … across vast oceans … and through detailed cities from Chicago to Tokyo.
  • Flight Simulator 2000 Professional Edition provides everything you need to be cleared for takeoff. State-of-the-art customization tools, charts, and data help you push the envelope.
  • Manage the systems—detailed instrument panels include the latest avionics and glass-cockpit displays.
  • Set your course with a built-in flight planner and track your position on a moving map.
  • With the simple click of a button, Flight Simulator re-creates the latest worldwide weather—including clouds, rain, thunderstorms, and wind—automatically.
